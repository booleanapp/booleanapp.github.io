What is a one question survey?

You ask a qualitative question about your product or service. We send this question on your behalf to your users by email. We record their responses and give you realtime reports.

Why only one test ?

Traditional surveys do not get a lot of responses because they are long and boring. Asking only one question will get you a truckload of responses.
