Pricing FAQs

Do I have to buy credits before sending surveys?

Yes. Credits are prepaid.

What happens when I run out of credits?

Your surveys will not be sent. You can buy additional credits from the account section. You also can setup auto-renew to automatically refill credits when they run out.

What type of payments do you accept?

We accept credit and debit cards from Visa, MasterCard, American Express, JCB, Discover and Diners Club. We do not accept PayPal.
